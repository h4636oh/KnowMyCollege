Know My College
We established this website with the aim of assisting students who have recently passed their common college entrance exams and find themselves in a state of confusion regarding which college to enroll in. We offer a holistic form that is filled with more choices than just placement or cutoff. Based on the information provided in this form, we rank colleges. Additionally, we provide access to our chatbot, which is designed to address any queries or concerns you may have regarding your choices.
