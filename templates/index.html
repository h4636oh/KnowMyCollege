Know My College
We established this website with the aim of assisting students who have recently passed their common college entrance exams and find themselves in a state of confusion regarding which college to enroll in.

We offer a holistic form that is filled with more choices than just placement or cutoff. Based on the information provided in this form, we rank colleges. Additionally, we provide access to our chatbot, which is designed to address any queries or concerns you may have regarding your choices.

FAQs
How does the website determine college rankings based on the preferences filled in the form?
The website uses a sophisticated algorithm that analyzes the preferences filled in by users on the form. These preferences could include campus placements, location preferences, campus size, extracurricular activities, and more. The algorithm then cross-references this information with data on colleges and universities to generate personalized rankings tailored to each user's unique preferences.
Can I trust the accuracy of the rankings provided by the website?
Yes, you can trust the accuracy of the rankings provided by the website. Our algorithm is open source and takes into account a wide range of factors and data points to ensure that the rankings are as accurate and relevant as possible. However, it's important to remember that rankings are just one aspect to consider when choosing a college, and individual research and campus visits are also highly recommended.
How does the AI chatbot assist users with their queries about colleges and the ranking process?
The AI chatbot on our website is designed to assist users with any questions they may have about colleges, the ranking process, or any other related queries. The chatbot uses natural language processing and machine learning to understand and respond to user inquiries in real-time, providing helpful information and guidance.
Are there any fees associated with using the website or accessing the college ranking information?
There are no fees associated with using our website or accessing the college ranking information. We believe in providing valuable resources and information to help students make informed decisions about their higher education options, free of charge.
Where can I ask more queries or seek further assistance if I have additional questions?
If you have more questions or need further assistance, you can utilize two main channels. Firstly, you can fill out the form located at the bottom of this page. Alternatively, you can reach out to us through our social media channels such as Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram. Your satisfaction and clarity are our top priorities, and we're dedicated to helping you make informed decisions about your higher education journey.
